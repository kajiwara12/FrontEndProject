WELCOME! This is my crude version of the card game Blackjack!
Rules:
Get a hand value higher than the dealer.
If you go over 21 you lose.
The dealer will draw cards until their
hand is greater than 17 when you press stay


How to start:
1.Press"New Deck" button.
2.Press "Deal Cards" button
3.To take another card press the "HIT" button
4.Press "STAY" button. Then the Dealer
will take their turn
5.To play again press "Deal Cards" again

Face card value:
KING = 10
QUEEN = 10
JACK = 10
